Father, Husband, Software Engineer who loves to code! Challenges? Bring 'em on, because `"It's possible"` &mdash; that's my motto.

# Softwares I use

## Productivity
- [1Password Families](https://1password.com/families/) - the best password manager that I have used to date.
- [Basecamp Personal](https://basecamp.com/personal) to organize my family and personal life
- [Hey](https://hey.com) for email. Made by creators of basecamp - check out their [mainfesto](https://hey.com/the-hey-way/)

## Coding
- [iTerm2](https://iterm2.com) - great replacement for Terminal with saved profile, etc
- [VSCode](https://code.visualstudio.com)
- [PyCharm](https://www.jetbrains.com/pycharm/) - I use PyCharm for work
- [Sublime Text](https://www.sublimetext.com) - I use this mainly as a powerful text editor and have unsaved files open for quick reference. Sublime is great because it reopens all unsaved documents if I ever restart Sublime / my laptop.

## Other
- Safari - I use Safari as my main browser. Would've used Firefox if I were on a GNU/Linux machine.
- Apple Notes to keep random shared notes
- [Dropbox](https://www.dropbox.com/individual) for my family storage needs
- [1.1.1.1](https://1.1.1.1/family/) - Orbi router's DNS is set to `1.1.1.3` to provide free and automatic malware protection for our home Internet
